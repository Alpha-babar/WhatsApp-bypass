## 📖 About This Project

This repository is dedicated to **raising awareness about WhatsApp phishing attacks**. With over **2 billion users** worldwide, WhatsApp has become a prime target for cybercriminals. This project aims to:

- Educate users on **how phishing works** on WhatsApp.
- Showcase **real-world examples** of phishing attempts.
- Provide **actionable steps** to stay safe.
- Share **resources** for reporting and recovery.

> ⚠️ **Disclaimer:** This project is for **educational purposes only**. The goal is to promote cybersecurity awareness and protect users from digital threats.

---

## 🎯 Why WhatsApp Phishing?

WhatsApp's **end-to-end encryption** gives users a false sense of complete security. However, phishing attacks exploit **human psychology**, not encryption. Attackers use:

- **Social engineering**
- **Fake links**
- **Impersonation of trusted contacts**
- **Fake giveaways & prizes**

> 📈 According to recent reports, **phishing attacks on messaging apps increased by 65%** in the last year.

---

## 🚨 Common Phishing Techniques

| Technique | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Fake Verification** | Scammers impersonate WhatsApp support asking for verification codes | "Your account will be deleted in 24 hours. Verify here: [fake-link]" |
| **Malicious Links** | Links disguised as offers, videos, or documents | "Claim your free $500 Walmart gift card! 🎁 [link]" |
| **Impersonation** | Hackers clone profile pictures and names of friends/family | "Hey, it's me. I lost my phone. Can you send me the OTP sent to your number?" |
| **Fake Job Offers** | Scammers lure victims with high-paying remote jobs | "Work from home! Earn $1000/week. Click here to apply." |
| **Voice Note Scams** | AI-generated voice notes mimicking family members asking for money | Fake distress calls requesting urgent funds |

## 🛡️ How to Protect Yourself

### ✅ Do's
- **Enable Two-Step Verification** in WhatsApp settings.
- **Verify suspicious messages** by calling the contact directly.
- **Check links** before clicking — use link preview or a URL scanner.
- **Update WhatsApp** regularly to get latest security patches.
- **Report phishing** attempts to WhatsApp immediately.

### ❌ Don'ts
- **Never share** your 6-digit verification code with anyone.
- **Don't click** on unsolicited links or download unknown attachments.
- **Don't trust** messages creating urgency or panic.
- **Avoid** scanning unknown QR codes.
